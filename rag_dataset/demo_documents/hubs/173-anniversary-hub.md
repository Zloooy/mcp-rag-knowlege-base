<h2 id="toc0"><span>On the 22nd of June, 2007, a user by the name of <em>S. S. Walrus</em> (later and more commonly known as <em>Moto42</em>) made the original SCP-173 post onto the /x/ board of 4chan. You can see an archived view of the post <a href="https://archive.ph/QD9UF">here</a>! This was the origin of SCP as we know it. 15 years and almost 7,000 SCP articles later with numerous tales, formats, and other kinds of pages, the SCP Foundation project is still going strong thanks to our vibrant community both on and off the site!</span></h2>
