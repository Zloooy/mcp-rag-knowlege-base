<p>12 Day's a Christmas (12DOC) is a 12 day anthology centered around Christmas and the winter holidays, inspired by the "12 Days of Christmas" Christmas carol. Our <em>tree-mendously</em> wonderful cast of authors have brewed up a 12 fantastic stories, each of various genres and themes, that <em>yule</em><sup class="footnoteref"><a id="footnoteref-1" href="javascript:;" class="footnoteref" onclick="WIKIDOT.page.utils.scrollToReference('footnote-1')">1</a></sup> be sure to love!</p>
