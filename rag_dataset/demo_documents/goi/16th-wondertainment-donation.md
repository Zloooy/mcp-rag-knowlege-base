<p><span style="font-size:130%;"><span style="color:#6BAB30;"><strong>Maintenance and Operation:</strong></span></span></p>
<ul>
<li><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">MAINTENANCE:</span></strong> No maintenance concerns noted.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">OPERATION:</span></strong> <strong>1)</strong> Open 'battery slot'. <strong>2)</strong> Introduce piece of edible food — preferably, <span style="text-decoration: underline;">unwrapped</span> candy, as it seems to be the intended 'power source' — in 'battery slot'. <strong>3)</strong> Establish a pattern of iterative thoughts related to the desired receipt or receipts of the communication until the Asset establishes said communication. <strong>4)</strong> Reload 'batteries' as necessary.</li>
</ul>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>ATTENTION:</strong> Communication can be established between any pair of units; however, batteries will run out faster if the receiving unit is not charged, and communication will not happen if attempted from an uncharged unit.</p>
</blockquote>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>WARNING:</strong> Repeated usage of the Asset invariably results in malfunction. A period of ten to thirteen hours of 'rest' between uses of ten to fifteen minutes, maximum, is advised.</p>
</blockquote>
<p><span style="font-size:130%;"><span style="color:#6BAB30;"><strong>Safety Concerns:</strong></span></span> Safe for all.</p>
<p><span style="font-size:130%;"><span style="color:#6BAB30;"><strong>Security Restrictions:</strong></span></span> Since no interception is possible when using the Sixteenth Wondertainment Donation to communicate, it is safe to say that this item does not require special Security Restrictions, as long as it is not identified as a means of long-range communications to persons unrelated to the Manna Charitable Foundation. Free agents and Executive members of the Manna Charitable Foundation Work Groups should be given individual items of the Sixteenth Wondertainment Donation, as Programme requirements state.</p>
<blockquote>
<p><strong>ATTENTION:</strong> Since there is no fixed schedule on the delivery of new batches, the International Board has full authority on the free agents and/or executive members that receive the Sixteenth Wondertainment Donation. Remember: this is a prized, <span style="text-decoration: underline;">rare</span> asset with a noticeable impact in our global activities. If you find one, report immediately to an Executive member.</p>
</blockquote>
